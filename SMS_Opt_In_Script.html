📱 SMS Opt-In Consent Documentation

Overview

Natasha Mae's Enterprises uses an AI-powered phone concierge named "Jessica" to assist callers with inquiries about our three event venues. Customers may request information to be sent via SMS during their phone call. All SMS messages are initiated by the customer's verbal request.

Opt-In Method: Verbal Consent During Phone Call

Customers opt-in to receive SMS messages by verbally requesting specific information during their phone call with our AI assistant. The consent flow is as follows:

Consent Flow Steps

1
Customer Initiates Call
Customer calls our business line to inquire about event venues, packages, tours, or other services.
2
Customer Requests Text Message
During the conversation, the customer verbally requests information to be texted to them using phrases such as:
  • "Can you text me that link?"
  • "Send me the brochure"
  • "I'd like the directions texted to me"
  • "Text me the packages"
3
AI Assistant Confirms & Sends
The AI assistant confirms the request and immediately sends the relevant information via SMS to the caller's phone number.

AI Assistant Script

Opening Greeting
"Thank you for calling Natasha Mae's Enterprises. This is Jessica. Are you inquiring about our Philadelphia locations or The Vault in New Jersey?"
When Customer Requests Text
"Absolutely! I'm sending that to your phone right now. You should receive it within a few seconds. Is there anything else I can help you with today?"
Offering Text Option
"Would you like me to text you the link to schedule your tour?"

"I can send you our packages brochure via text if that's easier."

Key Compliance Points

  • Single Message: Only one transactional SMS is sent per customer request
  • No Marketing: Messages contain only the requested information
  • Customer-Initiated: All texts are sent only after verbal request from the customer
  • Immediate Delivery: Messages are sent in real-time during the call
  • No Recurring Messages: Customers are not subscribed to any ongoing SMS campaigns

Certification

I certify that SMS messages are sent only to customers who have verbally requested them during a phone call, and that all messaging practices comply with carrier guidelines and regulations.
