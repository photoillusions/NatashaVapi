Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Natasha Mae's Enterprises ("we," "our," or "us") operates event venues in Philadelphia, PA and Burlington, NJ. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our services, website (www.natashamaes.com), or AI phone assistant.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Event Details: Event dates, guest counts, venue preferences
  • Phone Records: Call transcripts when you speak with our AI assistant
  • Communication Preferences: Your opt-in consent for SMS messages

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries about our venues
  • To schedule tours and process bookings
  • To send SMS messages you request (links, directions, invoices)
  • To send booking confirmations and event reminders
  • To improve our services and customer experience

4. SMS Messaging

When you call our business and verbally request information via text message, you consent to receive that specific SMS. Key points:

  • SMS messages are sent only when you request them
  • Messages contain only the information you requested
  • We do not send marketing texts without separate consent
  • Standard message and data rates may apply
  • Reply STOP to any message to opt out of future texts

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who help us operate (phone systems, SMS delivery)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

7. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of SMS communications

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
